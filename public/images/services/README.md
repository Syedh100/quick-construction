# Service Images

Place your service images in this folder. The following images are expected:

1. `carpentry.jpg` (or .png/.webp) - Image for Carpentry & Joinery service
2. `painting.jpg` (or .png/.webp) - Image for Painting & Decorating service
3. `tiling.jpg` (or .png/.webp) - Image for Tiling service

## Recommended Image Specifications

- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:3 or 16:10
- **Minimum Resolution**: 800x600px
- **Recommended Resolution**: 1200x900px
- **Format**: WebP preferred for best performance, JPG/PNG also supported

## Tips for Best Results

- Use high-quality, professional photos
- Ensure good lighting and composition
- Images should clearly represent each service
- Avoid overly busy backgrounds that might compete with text overlays
